🌍 Supporting Students Online & In Person

Be Selfless Uganda believes that distance should never be a barrier to education. Whether you are in Kampala, a rural village, or abroad, we are here to support you online and offline.

  • 💻 Connect with experienced mentors through BYU’s remote guidance programs, gaining personalized support wherever you are.
  • 📚 Access BYU’s curated online resources and e-learning tools designed to boost knowledge and practical skills.
  • 🤝 Join BYU’s vibrant peer-to-peer learning communities for collaboration, networking, and mutual growth.
  • 🎯 Receive personalized coaching from BYU educators to maximize academic performance and reach your goals.
  • 🌟 Apply for BYU-sponsored scholarships and programs specifically designed for remote learners, opening doors to higher education opportunities.

Our Students’ Joyful Gathering 🎄

On December 19, 2024, our dedicated online learners gathered for a joyful Christmas Celebration. After months of studying remotely, this moment was filled with laughter, faith, and unity. Together, they proved that education builds friendships, hope, and a brighter future.
