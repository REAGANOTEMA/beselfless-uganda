Committee Formation

Maraka William Stephen was elected Chairman of the Returned Missionaries Association, with William Agirai as Secretary and Proviah as Treasurer. His visionary leadership, exceptional organizational skills, and genuine commitment to youth development built a strong foundation for young Ugandans to thrive. Through his guidance, numerous programs were initiated to empower students, enhance community participation, and cultivate leadership skills. His ability to unite diverse groups, inspire volunteers, and foster collaboration exemplifies how effective mentorship can transform both individuals and communities over time.

Mukono Tech Center

Maraka personally spearheaded the builders to renovate the Mukono Tech Center, ensuring a welcoming environment for students and volunteers alike. From coordinating construction schedules, sourcing materials, to painting classrooms with his own hands, he demonstrated hands-on dedication. His tireless efforts transformed the space into a modern, inspiring hub for technology learning, mentorship workshops, and community engagement, creating countless opportunities for students to explore innovation, leadership, and personal growth in a supportive and vibrant setting.
