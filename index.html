About BeSelfless (U)

BeSelfless, incorporated on 2nd March 2023 by the Uganda Registration Service Bureau, is committed to nurturing resilient minds and empowering communities. Under the leadership of Stephen William Maraka, with support from Lanyero Oyul, Julius Aojan, and David Mulinda, the organization collaborates with Selfless Inc to sponsor BYU Pathway and Ensign College students. Through tech centers in Mukono, Masaka, and Lira, mentorship programs, and digital learning initiatives, the team provides students with tools for lifelong learning and leadership. Their efforts are focused on creating sustainable change, fostering self-reliance, and building a generation capable of transforming Uganda’s future. Learn more about our history.
