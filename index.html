About BeSelfless (U)

BeSelfless, incorporated on 2nd March 2023 by the Uganda Registration Service Bureau, is committed to nurturing resilient minds and empowering communities. Under the leadership of Stephen William Maraka, with support from Julius Aojan, the organization collaborates with Selfless Inc to sponsor BYU Pathway and Ensign College students. Through tech centers in Mukono, Masaka, and Lira, mentorship programs, and digital learning initiatives, the team provides students with tools for lifelong learning and leadership. Their efforts focus on creating sustainable change, fostering self-reliance, and building a generation capable of transforming Uganda’s future. Learn more about our history.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

Nurturing Resilient Minds, empowering students and communities to thrive in education, leadership, and life.

Mission

Believing in every life, Engaging with compassion, and Supporting efforts with love and persistence. Guiding families and individuals toward lifelong education and true self-sufficiency so hope is restored, dignity is protected, and no one is left behind.
