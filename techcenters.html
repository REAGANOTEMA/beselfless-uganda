Our Tech Centers

Freedom City Tech Center

This Freedom City-Kampala Tech Center provides hands-on IT training, coding workshops, and access to the latest technology, fostering innovation and preparing students to excel in digital careers. The center encourages collaboration and creativity among learners of all ages in the heart of the capital city.

The manager oversees daily operations, ensures smooth coordination of programs, supervises instructors, and maintains high educational standards. They also facilitate mentorship sessions, organize community events, and guide students in developing practical technology skills efficiently and effectively.

Students at this center engage in coding, robotics, and digital literacy programs that prepare them for technology-driven careers. Many students also participate online due to distance, ensuring inclusive access to education. Collaborative projects, workshops, and competitions help enhance problem-solving, teamwork, and creativity for professional growth.

Jinja Tech Center

Jinja Tech Center equips students with digital skills through advanced programming courses, hands-on workshops, and innovation labs. The center emphasizes practical learning and creative problem-solving, helping students become confident in modern technology and prepare for careers in IT and entrepreneurship.

The center manager coordinates daily activities, supervises instructors, and oversees program delivery. They implement mentorship programs, guide students on projects, and ensure quality education while fostering a positive and supportive learning environment for all participants.

Students participate in collaborative projects, coding challenges, and digital innovation workshops that enhance practical technology skills. Many learners join online due to distance, allowing wider access to quality education. The center focuses on creativity, critical thinking, and career readiness for future tech opportunities.

Lira Tech Center

Lira Tech Center provides students in Northern Uganda with advanced technology education, coding, and robotics programs. The center fosters innovation and problem-solving, preparing learners to contribute meaningfully to the digital economy and develop practical IT skills efficiently.

The manager ensures smooth operation of programs, mentors instructors, and supports student development. They coordinate workshops, supervise projects, and maintain high-quality technology education standards across the center, ensuring every student receives comprehensive guidance.

Students engage in coding, robotics, and digital skills programs, gaining hands-on experience. Many also study online due to distance. Collaborative learning, mentorship, and project work enhance critical thinking, creativity, and teamwork, equipping students for global technology opportunities.
