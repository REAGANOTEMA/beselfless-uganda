Be Selfless Uganda, incorporated on 2nd March 2023, nurtures resilient minds and empowers communities. Under the leadership of Stephen William Maraka and support from Lanyero Oyul, Julius Aojan, and David Mulinda, the organization collaborates with Selfless Inc to sponsor BYU Pathway and Ensign College students. Through tech centers in Mukono, Masaka, and Lira, mentorship programs, and digital learning initiatives, we provide tools for lifelong learning and leadership. Learn more about our history.
