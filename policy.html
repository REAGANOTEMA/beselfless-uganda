BESELFLESS Handbook Policies

1. Purpose and Mission

The mission of BeSELFLESS is to support efforts leading families and individuals toward lifelong education and self-sufficiency. We foster a safe, supportive learning environment empowering young adults to uplift their communities.

Board Members and Function

As of 11/1/2024, BeSELFLESS board members uphold organizational integrity. Each board member has one vote; in a tie, board advisors decide. Initiatives approved by BeSELFLESS Board require final approval by the SELFLESS Board.

New Applicant Qualification Requirements

Applicants must submit an application and receive BeSELFLESS Board approval. Requirements include:

  • Membership in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, or alternative conditions.
  • Age under 30 or turning 30 in joining year.
  • Single and childless upon entry; special conditions for married applicants.

Technology Center Major Expenditures

Expenditure details for Mukono, Jinja, Masaka, Freedom City, Ntinda, and Ura including size, rent, and owners.

Stipend & Transportation Requirements

Full-time and part-time student stipends, deductions, and attendance requirements are outlined for BYU-Idaho, Ensign College, and Pathway students.

Academic Probation Policy

Full-time and part-time students not meeting GPA/credit requirements are subject to probation, suspension, and stipend adjustments.

Disciplinary Action for Dropped Classes

Rules for tuition recovery and transportation reimbursements based on dropped credits and deadlines.

Honor Code Violations

Serious and minor violations with corresponding penalties ranging from probation to suspension or termination.

Tech Center Access Policy

Full-time students have priority access; part-time students have limited access. Computers require scheduled use and supervision.

Employee Compensation & Guidelines

Full-time and part-time directors, managers, tutors, and staff have set salaries and bonus structures. Employment length, performance rules, and termination policies are defined.

Expense Reimbursement Policy

Travel and meal reimbursements for full-time employees are allowed with receipts and proper reporting.

Internet and Security Policy

Strict monitoring and controlled internet access. Dormitory rules for students residing far from tech centers.
